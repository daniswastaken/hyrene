# Hyrene

**Hyrene** is a bold, uppercase display typeface originally created while designing promotional visuals for the **[Bedrock Perfected](https://mcpedl.com/bedrock-perfected/)** project.

It was born out of frustration.

While designing Bedrock Perfected update banner, I couldn’t find a font that perfectly matched the industrial, sharp, modern energy I needed. So instead of compromising… I made one.

And that’s how Hyrene happened.

---

## Origin Story

Hyrene is a fusion of:

- Hanken Grotesk  
- Poppins  
- Barlow  

With major structural modifications, spacing refinements, numeral reconstruction, and stylistic adjustments.

It’s not a simple remix — it’s a rebuilt display system designed specifically for strong, bold usage.

---

## Why “Hyrene”?

The name comes from:

- **Cyrene** (HSR character I genuinely love and adore)
- My own name, **Handaru**
- And because it starts with **H**

So I mixed them together and got:

**Hyrene**

Yes, it’s that simple. And yes, it’s intentional.

---

## Design Philosophy

Hyrene is:

- Uppercase-focused  
- Built for bold headlines  
- Industrial  
- Geometric but engineered  
- Designed for display, not body text  

It works best for:

- Branding  
- Posters  
- Headlines  
- Hero sections  
- Logo
